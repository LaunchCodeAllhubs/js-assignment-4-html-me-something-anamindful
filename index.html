Caterpillar: How?!
I am not a butterfly. I am just a caterpillar. You have me mistaken with someone else. I do not have wings that could make me fly.
I am low to the ground and I craw on my belly.
I could never fly. I am in the dirt. I am covered in mud. I am stuck on the ground, cant you see?
I will never have what it takes to fly.

Butterfly: I have wings, how could it be?!
You were right all along. I have wings to fly. I have wings! When you saw me crawling on the ground deep in despair you saw past my struggle and you saw past my pain. You saw me as I was created to be. When I was still searching for my true self. And when I felt lost and alone on the ground, you saw me as I was destined to be. I am fantastic, I am majestic, I am a free flying butterfly in the sky. Just as you made me to be. I am brave.
I am strong.
I am healthy.
And most assuredly I am loved! The journey was hard to become a butterfly but hard things always bring great joy! You knew my story from the start. You see me for who I am.

Author: You were always majestic my butterfly.
I loved you from the start. When you were crawling in the mud in your lowest low I loved you. When you were in your cocoon finding yourself I loved you. And now when you are flying in the sky with the colors of rainbows on your back I still love you. For you are loved from the beginning and always.
